The Retention Problem

Every lost subscriber costs 5-7x more to replace than to retain. Yet most operators still fight churn with blunt tools.

Margin Blind

"Save at any cost" destroys profitability. Agents give away the highest-value offer to every subscriber regardless of risk.

Integration Tax

Months of system integrator work for each new channel. By the time it's live, the business need has changed.
