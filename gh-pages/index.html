The Problem

Retention is broken.
Offers are guesswork.

Your agents improvise. Your margins erode. Compliance gaps widen. And 30% of at-risk subscribers leave within 72 hours of their first complaint.

Slow & Inconsistent

Agents read static scripts or improvise. Every call is different. No system decides which offer fits this subscriber right now.

Margin Blind

"Save the customer at any cost" destroys profitability. The most expensive offer always wins when margin isn't in the equation.

Integration Tax

Each channel — Agentforce, Genesys, your app — needs months of SI. By the time it's live, the market has moved.

The Solution

One API call.
Three ranked offers.

RetainIQ plugs into your existing channels and BSS. It doesn't replace Agentforce, Genesys, or your billing system — it sits between them and tells them which offer to present, with compliance and margin built in.
